The Main Characters:


Frisk:

Frisk is the character that the player plays as throughout Undertale. Frisk is the last of the eight humans to fall into the Underground after travelling and falling down a hole in Mount Ebott.


Flowey:

Flowey is the first major character the player encounters in Undertale. Flowey serves as the main antagonist for the majority of the game. He introduces the games "encounter" mechanics to the player by sharing "friendliness pellets" which turn out to be bullets, revealing his philosophy of "KILL or BE KILLED."


Toriel:

Toriel is the second character the player encounters in Undertale. She saves the player from Flowey and guides them through the Ruins. Her name is a pun of the word "tutorial" because she acts as the info to how the game works.


Sans:

Sans is the brother of Papyrus and first appears in Snowdin Forest after the player exits the Ruins. He plays the supporting character in the Neutral and Pacifist routes and as the final boss in the Genocide route.


Papyrus:

Papyrus is the brother of Sans and his main motive at the begining of the game is to capture a human so he could become a member of the Royal Guard. Later on befriends the player and plays as a supporting character.


Undyne:

Undyne is a fish-like monster that dawns a full suit of armour and is the leader of the Royal Guard. She pursues the player throughout all of Waterfall and in either evaded or thwarted by Monster Kid.


Alphys:

Alphys is the Royal Scientist and the player later on finds out that she had been following the player from the begining of the game. She is the creator of Mettaton and leads to the discovery of the True Lab, which lies underneath the Lab above in Hotlands.


Mettaton:

Mettaton is a robot with a Soul that was built by Alphys. Initially meant as entertainment later turned into a human killing robot. Mettaton isthe sole T.V star of the Underground.


Asgore:

Asgore Dreemurr is the ruler of the Underground and its denizens, monsters. He is the ex-husband of Toriel, father of Asriel Dreemurr and the adoptive father of the first human. Although he never had any malicious intent, he plays one of the main antagonists, planning to kill seven humans and use their Souls to break the barrier.


Asriel:

Asriel Dreemurr is the biological child of Toriel and Asgore. He plays as the the final boss of the Pacifist route.


Chara:

Chara, also known as the fallen human, is the first human to fall into the Underground.
